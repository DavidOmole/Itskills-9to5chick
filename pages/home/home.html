A STEP by STEP GUIDE on how to SUBSCRIBE to 9to5Chicks

NEW USERS

Visit the login page and click on the Get Started button.

Once on the Get Started page, scroll to the bottom of the page and you will see three payment plans. (Achiever, Star Chick and Titan Chick).

On any plan that feels good to you, click on the button that says Activate Plan . You will immediately be taken to a page that asks you to input your name, email and password . Once you are done, click on the Next button. At this point, you will be taken to a new screen where you have to decide whether you want to pay monthly or every six months (bianually) or every year (annually).

Once you mark the checkbox for the option of your choice, click on the Pay Now button that appears.

Now, a Paystack checkout page will popup on the screen. Please enter your card details and make your payment.

Once the payment has been verified by Paystack, you can continue with the rest of the onboarding process.

Existing Users

Once your subscription expires, if Paystack does not charge and thus, resubscribe you to the platform, you will have to manually resubscribe on the app. This step by step guide is for users who fall into the latter category.

When you login, you will be taken to a page that urges you to Reactivate your Account.

Once you click on that button, you will be redirected to a page, where you can select the plan you want to pay for (Achiever or Star).

For any plan that you feel is immediately best for you, click on Activate Plan .

A modal will then popup, asking you to decide between paying monthly or biannually or yearly.

Once you have selected your preferred option, click on the Pay Now button.

At this point, a Paystack checkout page will appear on the screen.

Please fill in your card details to make payment.

Once the payment is verified as successful, you will be allowed to continue using the app.
Click on your profile in the left side-bar menu, Then click on Subscription and upgrade your existing plan
You must have created at least one goal on the platform and you must have completed all of the onboarding steps.
